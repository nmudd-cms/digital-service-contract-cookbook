This is a list comprised of tasks you can use to build your next digital services contract. These tasks embrace the governance outlined in the U.S. Digital Services Playbook.

Each task is made up of three parts:

  1. A chunk of work you want the prospective contractor to do.
  2. Scenario(s) that you can gauge the proficiency of the prospective contractors.
  3. Performance metrics or SLAs that you can measure the success of how well the contractor can perform the task.

Go through the list below and check the tasks you need. When your finished you can print/save your customized contract language as a baseline for your contract.

If you'd like to contribute to the Digital Service Contract Cookbook, please go to our Github project.

DISCLAIMER: This project is a proof-of-concept and is NOT tied to a specific statement-of-work, but serves as a baseline for writing new digital service contracts.
