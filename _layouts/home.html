This is a list comprised of tasks you can use to build your next digital services contract. These tasks embrace the governance outlined in the U.S. Digital Services Playbook.

Each task is made up of three parts:

  1. A chunk of work you want the prospective contractor to do.
  2. Scenario(s) that you can gauge the proficiency of the prospective contractors during your selection process.
  3. Performance metrics or SLAs that you can measure the success of how well the contractor can perform the task after an award.

To get started, go through the list below and check the tasks you need or select from one of the below "recipes". When your finished you can print/save your customized contract language as a baseline for your contract.
